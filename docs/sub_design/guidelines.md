### Naming Conventions

- Do not use spaces. Instead use _ (underscore)
- Do not use "New" or "old" or "Updated" words in filenames (part names or drawing labels)
- Grouping parts by names. An example is below:
  - 01 (decks)
  - 02 (drum related)
  - 03 (injector related)
