## Managing Analtyics

- Create a Analytics center of excellence. 4 phases
    - Ph1: Standard governance processes
    - Ph2: Product and test infrastructure utility
    - Ph3: Service utility
    - Ph4: innovation authority and flexible resource pool
- RACI (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed) matrix. Building this will help define roles in the team
- Agile management is recommended. docs\ref\12_agile_management_principles.PNG
- Pilot projects help to learn (unknowns unknowns)
- Psychology:
    - Silo mentality (set up to failure)
    - Curse of  knowledge (dumb it down for customer)
    - Tunnel vision: predisposed to a favored outcome. Get 2nd opinion
    - Echo chamber: reinforce each other's opinion. Diverse team is required
    - Analysis paralysis: over analysis. more data slows decisions
    - Selective Exposure. Only look at data that makes sense
    - Big Solution is Active listening: Reflective listening
