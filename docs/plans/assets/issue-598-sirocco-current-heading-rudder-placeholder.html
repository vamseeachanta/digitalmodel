Method summary

Local current-frame force components are calculated first, then rotated into ship-fixed components for charting. Positive Y_ship and N_ship indicate port sway force and bow-to-port yaw moment.

V = current_speed_kn × 0.51444 F = β × A_R × V² × C_r α = δ - ψ F_n = F × sin(α) X_local = F × sin²(α) Y_local = F × sin(α) × cos(α) X_ship = X_local × cos(ψ) - Y_local × sin(ψ) Y_ship = X_local × sin(ψ) + Y_local × cos(ψ) N_ship = Y_ship × yaw_lever_m

Chart interpretation

  • Force component chart: supports quick reading of surge/sway component magnitudes as heading changes for a selected rudder angle.
  • Yaw moment heatmap: shows full heading × rudder interaction for the selected speed, including zero-response bands where δ approximately equals ψ under the simplified relative-inflow model.
  • Dropdown behavior: all requested current speeds are available, with 4.56 kn selected by default for reviewer convenience.
