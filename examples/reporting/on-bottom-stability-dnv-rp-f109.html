Methodology

On-bottom stability of a concrete-coated subsea pipeline using the simplified method per DNV-RP-F109.

Standard: DNV-RP-F109

Equation eq1: Submerged weight
$$W_s = W_{dry} - \rho_{sw} g \frac{\pi}{4} D_{total}^2$$
Dry weight of steel + coating + contents minus buoyancy
(eq1)
Equation eq2: Drag force
$$F_D = \frac{1}{2} \rho_{sw} C_D D_{total} U |U|$$
Morison drag force per unit length
(eq2)
Equation eq3: Inertia force
$$F_I = \rho_{sw} C_M \frac{\pi}{4} D_{total}^2 a$$
Morison inertia force per unit length
(eq3)
Equation eq4: Lift force
$$F_L = \frac{1}{2} \rho_{sw} C_L D_{total} U^2$$
Hydrodynamic lift force per unit length
(eq4)
Equation eq5: Lateral stability utilization
$$\eta = \frac{\gamma (F_D + F_I)}{\mu (W_s - F_L)}$$
Stable when eta <= 1.0
(eq5)
