Methodology

Portfolio beta via OLS regression — measures systematic risk relative to benchmark

Standard: CAPM / Modern Portfolio Theory

Equation eq1: Beta
$$\beta = \frac{Cov(r_i, r_b)}{Var(r_b)}$$
Slope of regression of asset returns on benchmark returns
(eq1)
Equation eq2: Jensen's Alpha
$$\alpha = \bar{r_i} - \beta \bar{r_b}$$
Excess return not explained by benchmark
(eq2)
Equation eq3: R-squared
$$R^2 = 1 - \frac{SS_{res}}{SS_{tot}}$$
Proportion of variance explained by benchmark
(eq3)
