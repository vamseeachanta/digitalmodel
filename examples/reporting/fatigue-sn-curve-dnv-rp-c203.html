Methodology

Fatigue damage assessment using S-N curves per DNV-RP-C203. Determines allowable cycles N from the bi-linear S-N curve for a given stress range, then computes Miner's rule damage accumulation. The assessment includes thickness correction for plate thicknesses exceeding the reference.
