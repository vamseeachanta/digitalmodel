Methodology

Simplified absolute stability check for a subsea pipeline on a sandy seabed. Morison equation for hydrodynamic drag, inertia, and lift loads on a near-seabed pipe. Lateral stability requires pipe submerged weight to resist horizontal hydrodynamic forces via soil friction, with an appropriate safety factor.
