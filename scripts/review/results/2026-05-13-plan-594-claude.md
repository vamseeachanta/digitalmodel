## CRITICAL

**C1. Retained digitized competitor numerical data is not addressed by the boundary plan.**
- Plan section "Required changes" (Task 2) preserves `headline_metrics` (17.8 %, 25.9 %, 8.2 pp, 46 %, 15.3 pp) and the full `monthly_operability_climatology` table — values digitized from a SeaOps infographic that the issue context now flags as competitor-adjacent.
- The "Competitive context" guidance in `references/vessel-suitability/README.md:25-32` says "avoid copying competitor wording, layouts, or client-facing claims into AceEngineer deliverables." Digitized values from a competitor infographic are arguably *claims*; the plan only treats wording/layout as the bounded surface.
- The user's instruction in the review prompt was explicit: "It must not be promoted to GTM/client/project collateral unless explicitly approved later." The dataset is currently *committed to the repo*, available to any consumer that imports it, with no flag preventing client/report export beyond a prose note.
- **Fix:** add a sub-task to either (a) gate the digitized numerical values behind a `confidence_class: "rights_unresolved_no_external_use"` field plus a test that fails if a known consumer renders these to a client artifact, or (b) regenerate the operability climatology from public NORA3 hindcast (publicly licensed) and demote the SeaOps-derived numbers to "comparison only," or (c) defer the data-retention decision to a separate approved issue and remove the digitized numbers from the JSON until that decision lands.
